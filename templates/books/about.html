Why Pustakam?

Millions of books end up in landfills every year while students struggle to afford education. Pustakam bridges this gap. We are more than a marketplace; we are a movement for sustainable education.

By circulating used books, we reduce paper waste, save trees, and make learning accessible to everyone.
